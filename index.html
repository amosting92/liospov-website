About

Why this exists

Lio’s POV started with a simple realization: some of the most meaningful conversations don’t happen on a stage or behind a microphone. They happen during quiet walks, long drives, sleepless nights, or in the middle of an ordinary day.

This isn’t a place built on having all the answers. It’s a space for thinking out loud, sharing lessons while they’re still unfolding, and documenting the journey with honesty. If even one story helps someone pause, reflect, or see life from a different perspective, then it has served its purpose.

Whether it’s a podcast episode, a Project 365 journal entry, or a Sunday Reset reflection, every piece of content comes from the same belief: growth doesn’t always happen through big moments. Sometimes, it begins with one honest conversation.
